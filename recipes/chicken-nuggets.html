Chicken Nuggets

Description

Chicken nuggets are a personal favorite of mine, so being able to easily make a fresh batch whenever I want is a must! This recipe will absolutely blow your socks off with just how delicious it is. Make this, get your personal favorite drink and condiments, and go to town on these absolutely lovely nuggets.

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil for frying
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 6 tablespoons garlic salt
  • 3 tablespoons ground black pepper
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 8 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into small chunks

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Heat 1 inch oil in a large skillet or saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Stir together flour, garlic salt, and pepper in a bowl. Dip chicken pieces into beaten eggs, then press each piece into flour mixture to coat well; shake off excess flour. Place coated chicken pieces onto a plate.
  3. Working in batches, fry chicken in hot oil until golden brown and no longer pink in the center.
  4. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C). Serve and enjoy!
